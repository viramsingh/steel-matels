Hi Tech's Response to COVID-19 Pandemic

At Hi Tech, we are committed to safeguarding the health and well-being of our employees, clients, and the community during these challenging times posed by the COVID-19 pandemic.

We have implemented a comprehensive response plan to mitigate the spread of the virus and ensure business continuity. Our priority remains the safety of our workforce while continuing to serve our valued clients.

Here are some key elements of our response plan:

  • Regular health and temperature checks for all employees and visitors upon entry to our facilities.
  • Mandatory mask-wearing and social distancing measures in all workplace areas.
  • Remote work options for employees where feasible to reduce on-site presence.
  • Enhanced cleaning and sanitation protocols for all workspaces and common areas.
  • Providing employees with necessary personal protective equipment (PPE) and promoting its proper use.
  • Encouraging virtual meetings and limiting non-essential travel to minimize exposure risks.

We understand the gravity of the situation and are actively monitoring the COVID-19 developments. Our commitment to hygiene and safety is unwavering, and we are taking proactive measures to ensure a secure working environment for our employees.

Hi Tech's Response to COVID-19 Pandemic

At Hi Tech, we are committed to safeguarding the health and well-being of our employees, clients, and the community during these challenging times posed by the COVID-19 pandemic.

We have implemented a comprehensive response plan to mitigate the spread of the virus and ensure business continuity. Our priority remains the safety of our workforce while continuing to serve our valued clients.

Our response plan includes the following key measures:

  • Regularly monitoring and following guidelines issued by local health authorities and the World Health Organization (WHO).
  • Enforcing strict hygiene and sanitation protocols at all our facilities and workspaces.
  • Facilitating remote work options for our employees to reduce physical presence in the office.
  • Implementing social distancing measures in all common areas and meeting spaces.
  • Providing our employees with personal protective equipment (PPE) and necessary resources to stay safe.

Our Measures and Initiatives

Employee Hygiene and Safety

We prioritize employee health by promoting social distancing and providing information about hygiene practices.

Travel Restrictions

Non-essential travel is restricted, and we encourage virtual meetings to reduce exposure risks.

Remote Work

Where feasible, our employees work from home to minimize physical presence in offices.

Plant Operations

We maintain essential manufacturing operations with strict adherence to government guidelines.

Employee Well-being

For employees unable to work from home, we have implemented rigorous safety measures:

  • Installed sanitization tunnels and hands-free sanitizer dispensers.
  • Conduct daily health screenings for all employees.
  • Organize awareness programs and engage medical professionals.
  • Regularly sanitize workplaces, staff buses, and common areas.
  • Enforce the use of face masks, PPEs, and social distancing.
  • Prohibit gatherings and ensure hygiene and safety in all facilities.
  • Provide lock-in facilities with essential amenities.

Corporate Social Responsibility

We are committed to supporting our community:

  • Contributed to the 'Chief Minister’s Relief Fund'.
  • Provided assistance to District Health Centers and Self-Help Groups.

Our commitment to health, safety, and well-being extends beyond our workforce, and we will continue to take initiatives to support our society in the fight against COVID-19.
